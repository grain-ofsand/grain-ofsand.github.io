Making a Commission

All of our commissions are done through our discord server.

Follow the directions below the use the discord bot and join the server:

  1. Read our Prices and Agree to our TOS.
  2. Join our Discord Server!
  3. Run !c in #commands and answer the questions from the bot.
  4. Give a longer description of your art in the new commission channel.
